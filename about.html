ONI MOTOR SPARE PARTS

ONI Motor Spare Parts Company is a standard and registered auto spares parts retail store business that is located in one of the busiest market in Ladipo – Lagos, Nigeria. We have a facility that is big enough and also fit the design of the kind of standard Motor spare parts and the facility is located around the largest residential estate in Ladipo – Lagos Nigeria.

ONI Motor Spare Parts Company will be involved in selling automotive parts and accessories, distributing automotive parts from different manufacturers (brands) from the United States and abroad and repairing automobile. We are set to services a wide range of clientele in and around Ladipo market, Lagos-Nigeria.

We are aware that there are several large and small chains of auto spare parts retail store outlets all around Ladipo market, Lagos-Nigeria, which is why we spent time and resources to conduct a thorough feasibility studies and market survey so as to be well positioned to favorably compete with all our competitors. We have an online – service option for our customers, and our office is well secured with various payments of options.

ONI Motor Spare Parts Company will ensure that all our customers are given first class treatment whenever they visit our auto parts retail store. We have a CRM software that will enable us manage a one on one relationship with our customers no matter how large the numbers of our customers’ base may grow to. We will ensure that we get our customers involved in the selection of brands that will be in our Company and also when making some business decisions.

We will ensure that we hold ourselves accountable to the highest standards by meeting our customers ‘needs precisely and completely whenever they patronize our products. We will also cultivate a working environment that provides a human, sustainable approach to earning a living, and living in our world, for our partners, employees and for our customers.

ONI Motor Spare Parts Company®, Is a business that is owned by Onyeka Nnaemeka and his immediate family members. Onyeka Nnaemeka with over 15 years of experience in the Motor Spare Parts industry, working for some of the leading brand in Nigeria. Although the business has one Company in Ladipo, Lagos-Nigeria, but there is a plan to open other Company all around major cities in Nigeria and Abroad.
